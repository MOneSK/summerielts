Study Plan

Follow this structured 45-day plan to improve your skills.

Week Focus Activities Duration
Weeks 1–2 Understand IELTS criteria, identify weaknesses, and build consistent study habits Writing:
- Analyze Band 7+ sample essays from IELTS Liz and IELTS Simon.
- Practice writing introductions and conclusions for Task 2 essays.
- Work on sentence structures and grammar accuracy. 		30 min/day
Speaking:
- Practice Part 1 and Part 2 questions using IELTS Speaking Topics.
- Record responses and self-evaluate for fluency and coherence.
- Focus on expanding answers with reasons and examples. 		30 min/day
Reading & Listening:
- Read articles from BBC Learning English to enhance vocabulary.
- Listen to TED Talks and summarize main points. 		15 min each/day
Weeks 3–4 Intensive practice with feedback and time management Writing:
- Write full Task 1 and Task 2 essays under timed conditions.
- Seek feedback from teachers or online forums.
- Implement advanced grammar structures and cohesive devices. 		45 min/day
Speaking:
- Engage in mock interviews with a study partner or tutor.
- Practice Part 3 questions, focusing on abstract topics.
- Work on pronunciation, intonation, and stress patterns. 		45 min/day
Reading & Listening:
- Complete practice tests from IELTS Practice Tests.
- Review incorrect answers to understand mistakes. 		15 min each/day
Weeks 5–6 Simulate test conditions and refine strategies Writing:
- Alternate between Task 1 and Task 2 essays.
- Focus on clarity, coherence, and task response.
- Review and revise essays based on feedback. 		1 hr/day
Speaking:
- Participate in full speaking tests with a tutor or language exchange partner.
- Work on extending answers and using a range of vocabulary.
- Record sessions to identify areas for improvement. 		1 hr/day
Reading & Listening:
- Focus on sections where you previously made errors.
- Practice under timed conditions to improve speed and accuracy. 		15 min each/day
Week 7 Consolidate knowledge, review mistakes, and prepare mentally for the exam Writing & Speaking:
- Review all previous essays and speaking recordings.
- Identify recurring mistakes and work on correcting them.
- Practice with a focus on fluency and coherence. 		1 hr/day
Reading & Listening:
- Complete one full reading and listening test.
- Analyze performance and focus on time management. 		30 min each/day
